# References

## Primary Literature

### 1. Kitaev (2001)

Kitaev, A. Y.

"Unpaired Majorana fermions in quantum wires."

Physics-Uspekhi, Volume 44, Number 10S, 2001.

DOI:
https://doi.org/10.1070/1063-7869/44/10S/S29

This paper introduced the Kitaev chain model and demonstrated the existence of Majorana zero modes in one-dimensional superconductors.

---

### 2. Alicea (2012)

Alicea, J.

"New Directions in the Pursuit of Majorana Fermions in Solid State Systems."

Reports on Progress in Physics, 2012.

DOI:
https://doi.org/10.1088/0034-4885/75/7/076501

A comprehensive review of Majorana fermions in condensed matter systems and their potential applications in quantum computation.

---

### 3. Beenakker (2013)

Beenakker, C. W. J.

"Search for Majorana Fermions in Superconductors."

Annual Review of Condensed Matter Physics, 2013.

DOI:
https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-conmatphys-030212-184337

Provides an overview of Majorana physics and experimental efforts to realize Majorana states.

---

## Topological Quantum Computation

### 4. Nayak et al. (2008)

Nayak, C.,
Simon, S. H.,
Stern, A.,
Freedman, M.,
Das Sarma, S.

"Non-Abelian Anyons and Topological Quantum Computation."

Reviews of Modern Physics, 2008.

DOI:
https://doi.org/10.1103/RevModPhys.80.1083

Foundational review on topological quantum computation and non-Abelian quasiparticles.
